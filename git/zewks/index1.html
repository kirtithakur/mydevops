About Me

I have been intrigued with reading children’s books ever since I began to read as a toddler. Ever since, I have been reading and studying children’s books. I then became interested in elementary education. I received a Bachelor’s Degree in Elementary Education at Bridge Water State University and a Master’s Degree in Elementary Education at Salem State University in Massachusetts. I am now pursuing a Doctorate’s Degree in Elementary Education.

Zewks.com

Is a website which sells entertainment, educational, non-violent children’s books and videos. This is a juvenile education website meant to provide Entertainment for kids and family.

Star-Kids: Solar System Exploration

Zooming through the universe faster than the speed of light, exploring the Milkyway Galaxy and our solar system, these 10 students narrowly escape comets, meteors and all the outerspace dangers. Barely escaping with their lives on many occasions, read what other perils these kids face.

Claymates Presents George Washington

Claymates Presents George Washington is a story about the life of the general of the Continental Army who claimed victory over England by winning the American Revolutionary War, then eventually becoming the first President of the United States of America.See how these eight clay playmates (Claymates) read his biography as they imagine themselves portraying a certain character each, as every chapter is being read. Filled with thirty-three dimensional pictures who portray colonial figures give you a clear picture of how it wasback then.

Historical-Rhetorical: Father of his Country

Read how one of America's most famous citizens rose to fame. Written in poetic verse and told in first person, this 32 page book begins with his joyful birth, continues with his playful childhood, famous political career, involvement in the French & Indian War, appointment to General of the Continental Army, election to first President of the United States, then ends with his much deserved retirement.
